About

  • github
  • LinkedIn

I pursued three simultaneous degrees in computer engineering and electrical engineering from 2016 to 2023. I was admitted to Iran’s second-ranked technical university, Amirkabir University of Technology, in 2016 to study Electrical Engineering. Due to being ranked first among the students, I was granted permission to pursue a second degree in Computer Engineering as well. In 2020, I won the gold medal in the National Olympiad for Electrical Engineering, which enabled me to pursue my master’s degree at the University of Tehran in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, specializing in the Smart Networks Laboratory. Ive received several awards, including Dual Degree and Dual Level Honors, and the Gold Medal in the National Electrical Engineering Olympiad. Additionally, I was awarded a full scholarship for an AI internship at Tsinghua University, China. I ranked 1st in the 6th Young Scientists Festival in Math and CS, and was recognized as a UNESCO Distinguished Young Scientist last year.
