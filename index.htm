I'm Mohammad Hossein Badiei

I am Mohammad Hossein Badiei, a dual degree student in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering. I have earned three academic degrees simultaneously, just over six years, including two Bachelor's degrees of CE and EE from Tehran Polytechnic and a Master's degree of EE from Tehran University. During my short academic journey, I achieved some milestones, such as ranking 9th in the 2021 National EE Olympiad and securing 1st place in Mathematics and Computer Science at the 2022 Young Scientists International Festival. Additionally, I earned top honors in my BS major at the University of Tehran Polytechnic, receiving Simultaneous Dual Degree Honors. My current research encompasses robust deep learning, adversarial machine training, generalized federated learning, algorithm design, and generative AI. I also apply generative AI methodologies to tackle practical challenges in applied physics and mathematics. In 2022, I founded a software group with the goal of enhancing printing services through a fully automated driverless printing platform, which you can access at http://cafecartridge.com/ (Now in Trial Mode). This initiative aimed to streamline and improve the printing process through advanced software solutions. Furthermore, I currently serve as a team leader in multiple research groups in Iran and South Korea that focus on developing algorithms for federated learning, deep learning, adversarial training, quantum learning, computer vision, financial engineering, and robotics. My current interests encompasses robust deep learning, generalized federated learning, adversarial training, computer vision, generative AI, GNNs, applied physics and mathematics, AR/VR, robotics, NLP, and ML in medical and financial applications :)
